Nonprofit & Government Summit

Date: Friday, August 14, 2020
Time: 8:30am - 12:00pm
Location: Virtual / Online

The Nonprofit & Government Summit is a space for nonprofit, government and activist technologists to come together and learn how to make the most of their Drupal websites and online presence in general.

Come network and expand your skill set through lightning talks, breakout discussions, panels and tech clinics.
